To contribute to supporting units and partners to improve the quality of cyber security human resources, Vietnam Information Security Association (VNISA) in collaboration with RMIT organizes the training program “Improving awareness of cyber security” for information security and IT officers in organizations and businesses.

The first course in the program will take place in 1 day, providing students with general knowledge about information security awareness for small and medium enterprises. After the course, students are granted a Certificate of course completion from VNISA and RMIT.

  • Time: 9:00 – 13:00 Friday, December 8, 2023.
  • Location: Movenpick Living West Hanoi Hotel, 21 Duy Tan, My Dinh, Cau Giay, Hanoi.

In order for the organization to be thoughtful, it is recommended that agencies and businesses that have received invitations from VNISA please send registration information to the VNISA Office before December 5, 2023.
